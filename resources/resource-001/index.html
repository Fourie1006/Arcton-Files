RESOURCE 001 — PATHFINDING TOOLS

COMPANION: FILE 001 • STATUS: PUBLIC RELEASE

This resource is designed to be practical.
Use it exactly as written.
Copy the templates. Replace the variables. Run the searches. Collect evidence.

SECTION A — SELF-ASSESSMENT (TRAIT-BASED FIRST)

Start with language. Not labels. Track tendencies. Then test reality.

  • O*NET Interest Profiler — Open Tool →
    Maps interests across real work domains.
  • VIA Character Strengths — Open Tool →
    Identifies core strengths like curiosity, empathy, discipline.

Note on “type” tests:
They can be motivating, but often lock you into categories. If you want something more flexible, focus on OCEAN-style traits (you move along a spectrum, not into a box).

Arcton Trait Survey (OCEAN-aligned):
Begin Survey →

Suggested survey intro (paste into Google Forms):
“You are not being tested. This survey maps tendencies, not limits. Answer honestly, not aspirationally.”

Suggested confirmation message:
“Your responses have been logged. No profile is final. Traits shift with experience. Continue observing patterns.”

SECTION B — CAREER REALITY CHECK (WHAT THE JOB ACTUALLY IS)

  • CareerOneStop — Open →
    Role summaries, skills, and pathways (broad coverage).
  • BLS Occupational Outlook Handbook — Open →
    Job growth, pay ranges, requirements, common conditions.
  • Prospects (UK) — Open →
    Early-career expectations, routes in, day-to-day framing.

SECTION C — HIGH-QUALITY AI PROMPTS (COPY/PASTE)

Use these to force specificity. Replace [JOB TITLE] and [COUNTRY] only.

PROMPT 01 — ROLE REALITY You are an experienced [JOB TITLE] with at least 5 years in the field. Describe: - a realistic day-to-day workflow (hour-by-hour if possible) - the 5 skills that matter most (not generic) - the 5 stressors people underestimate - what you wish schools explained about this role Avoid vague descriptions. PROMPT 02 — FIT CHECK (TRAITS → TASKS) Given these traits: - Openness: [LOW/MED/HIGH] - Conscientiousness: [LOW/MED/HIGH] - Extraversion: [LOW/MED/HIGH] - Agreeableness: [LOW/MED/HIGH] - Neuroticism: [LOW/MED/HIGH] Evaluate whether [JOB TITLE] is a good fit. Be honest. Include risks and compensating strategies. PROMPT 03 — PATHWAY MAP Outline a realistic pathway to become a [JOB TITLE] in [COUNTRY]. Include: - subjects to prioritize now - degree options (if relevant) - beginner projects / volunteering to test the role within 14 days - a 90-day exploration plan that builds evidence, not vibes

SECTION D — WARM OUTREACH TEMPLATE (WITH VARIABLES)

Subject: Quick question about your work as a [JOB TITLE] Hi [NAME], Hope your week’s going well. I’m currently exploring possible study and career paths, and I came across your work as a [JOB TITLE]. I’m trying to understand what the role looks like in real life — the day-to-day tasks, the skills that matter most, and what you enjoy (and what’s tougher than people expect). If you’re open to it, could I ask you a few quick questions? I can do it by message/email, or we could do a short 10-minute call — whatever is easiest for you. If there’s any specific area you think I should focus on (e.g., [SKILL 1], [SKILL 2], [TOOL/AREA]), I’d really appreciate your guidance. Either way, thanks for your time — and respect for the work you do. Best, [YOUR NAME] [OPTIONAL: SCHOOL / CITY]

ARCHON NOTE: people are more willing to help when you ask for clarity, not favors.
